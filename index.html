I Seen It

This one time in band camp, a flying frog-cat came outta nowhere and slapped me in the forehead. I did not know what to do so I just chilled on the beach and started skipping rocks.

"That's cray," is all I had to say. I don't remember much else, maybe that didn't even happen. I don't even know what a frog cat is.

I walked away from band camp without learning anything. Life is random and there is truly nothing to make of it.
